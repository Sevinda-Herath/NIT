About Us

Welcome to the ultimate Esports showdown on campus! Organized by the 2023 DT Batch, our exclusive tournament brings inner-faculty students together to compete in top mobile and PC games. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a casual gamer, this is your chance to showcase your skills, win prizes, and represent your faculty. Get ready for an action-packed gaming experience filled with competition, excitement, and camaraderie!
